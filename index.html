Top Golfers Memory Game

How to play

  • To start the game, click on any card to flip it over.
  • Only 2 cards can be flipped at a time.
  • Cards will return to original position if match is not made
  • 10 matches are required to complete the game
  • The challenge is to beat your best time, or quickest numbers of flips.
